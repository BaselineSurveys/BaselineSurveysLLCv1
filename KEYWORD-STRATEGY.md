## Where Keywords Are Placed

### Page Titles (SEO Impact: Very High)
✅ All pages have target keywords in titles
Example: "Summit County Surveyors | Land Surveys Breckenridge, Frisco, Keystone"

### Meta Descriptions (SEO Impact: High)
✅ All pages have keyword-rich descriptions
Example: "Baseline Surveys - Summit County surveyors offering land surveys in Breckenridge, Frisco, Silverthorne..."

### H1 Headings (SEO Impact: Very High)
✅ Updated on all key pages:
- Home: "Summit County Surveyors & Land Survey Services"

## Quick Reference: Keyword Placement

```
Title Tag:        "Summit County Surveyors | Land Surveys [City]"
Meta Description: "Baseline Surveys - [City/County] surveyors offering [service] surveys"
H1:              "[City/County] Land Surveys | Professional Surveyors"
H2:              "Service Name in [City/County]"
Content:         Natural mentions throughout (1-3%)
URL:             /city-service-surveys/ (if creating new pages)
Alt Text:        "[city] [service] surveyor [action]"
```

## Important Notes

1. **Consistency is Key** - Keep NAP (Name, Address, Phone) identical everywhere
2. **Quality Over Quantity** - 1 good piece of content beats 10 thin pages
3. **User Experience First** - Write for humans, keywords second
4. **Local Focus** - Emphasize your Colorado expertise and locations
5. **Reviews Matter** - Actively encourage and respond to customer reviews
