Land Survey Services in Summit County & Beyond

Professional surveying solutions for Breckenridge, Frisco, Silverthorne, Keystone, Dillon, Kremmling, Granby, Fairplay, and surrounding Colorado areas

Surveying Services Offered

ALTA/ACSM Land Survey

Comprehensive surveys meeting national standards, often required for commercial transactions, including title and lender requirements.

Improvement Survey

Detailed surveys showing boundary lines and the location of existing improvements including structures, utilities, and more.

Improvement Location Certificates (ILCs)

Typically required by lenders or building departments. These show building footprints and basic property information, but do not set monuments.

Topographic Surveys

Mapping of natural and man-made land features such as trees, elevation changes, driveways, and wetlands—used in design and permitting.

Platting

Subdivision, resubdivision, amended plats, and condominium mapping for land development and municipal approval processes.

Lot Consolidation & Lot Line Adjustments

Survey and mapping services to combine or reconfigure existing parcels of land, including legal documentation for recordation.

Construction Layout & Staking

Full layout services for site development projects, including structures, roads, and infrastructure elements.

  • Utilities – sewer, electric, water, storm, gas, communication
  • Foundations – including SILCs
  • Site grading and road layout
  • Retaining walls
  • OWTS – septic systems, leech fields, wells
  • Existing condition verification
